Where Talented People
Come to Grow

Our team is made up of some of the best clinicians and businesspeople in the veterinary profession. United Veterinary Care is a place where people come to practice what they’re passionate about — and change the world while they do.

Clinical Positions

We are looking for passionate doctors, practice managers, veterinary technicians, and customer service representatives to make a difference for pet parents.

Support our doctors, practice managers, and hospital team members as a Support Center Team member from our offices in sunny South Florida, in a field-based role, or in a remote role.
