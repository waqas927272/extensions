Doctor's Assistant for Veterinarians

Los Angeles Animal Specialty Emergency and Rehabilitation (LAASER) - Los Angeles, CA
$18.50 - $22.00 an hour
Apply Now

Los Angeles Animal Specialty, Emergency & Rehabilitation (LAASER) is seeking a motivated, dependable Veterinary Doctor’s Assistant (DA) to join our Emergency & Critical Care team. This is a fast-paced, high-acuity environment where teamwork, communication, and patient care come first.

About LAASER

LAASER is a multi-specialty emergency and referral hospital dedicated to providing advanced, compassionate care to our patients and exceptional support to our clients and referring veterinarians. Our team includes board-certified specialists and experienced ER doctors who collaborate closely to deliver high-quality medicine.

Position Overview

As a Doctor’s Assistant in the ER, you will serve as a key liaison between clients and doctors, helping ensure clear communication, efficient patient flow, and a supportive client experience during stressful and time-sensitive situations.

This role is ideal for someone who is comfortable speaking with clients, can explain treatment plans clearly, and thrives in an emergency environment.

The position is part-time and the schedule for this position will be Saturday, Sunday, Monday, Tuesday from 3pm -9pm.

Responsibilities include, but are not limited to:

  • Assisting doctors and technicians during exams, diagnostics, treatments, and procedures
  • Acting as a liaison between clients and doctors to relay medical information accurately and compassionately
  • Creating, reviewing, and presenting treatment estimates to clients
  • Discussing treatment options and obtaining client consent
  • Collecting deposits and processing client payments in accordance with hospital protocols
  • Supporting emergency intake and triage flow
  • Safely restraining and monitoring patients
  • Preparing exam rooms, treatment areas, and supplies
  • Maintaining accurate medical and financial documentation
  • Supporting overall ER workflow in a high-volume environment

Requirements:

  • Previous experience as a Veterinary Assistant or Doctor’s Assistant preferred
  • ER, specialty, or high-volume hospital experience strongly preferred
  • Strong communication and client service skills
  • Comfort discussing treatment plans, estimates, and financial expectations
  • Ability to work in a fast-paced, high-stress emergency environment and respond calmly during urgent and emotionally charged situations
  • Ability to communicate clearly, professionally, and compassionately with clients, doctors, and team members
  • Strong attention to detail and ability to follow written and verbal medical instructions accurately
  • Ability to multitask, prioritize responsibilities, and make sound decisions under pressure
  • Ability to stand, walk, bend, kneel, crouch, and reach for extended periods
  • Ability to safely lift and carry up to 50 pounds, and assist with lifting larger patients as a team
  • Ability to safely restrain animals of varying sizes, temperaments, and medical conditions
  • Ability to work in close proximity to animals, including those that may be fearful, aggressive, or in pain
  • Ability to tolerate exposure to animal bodily fluids, medical odors, cleaning agents, and loud or stressful environments
  • Ability to maintain focus and accuracy during extended shifts, including nights, weekends, and holidays
  • Basic computer proficiency, including the ability to enter medical records, create estimates, and process payments
  • Ability to maintain professionalism, emotional resilience, and situational awareness in a high-acuity ER setting

We welcome applications from candidates whose qualifications and experience align with this position.

Pay: $18.50 - $22.00 per hour

Benefits:

  • Employee discount

Work Location: In person
