Description

Position at Central Texas Veterinary Specialty & Emergency Hospital- Round Rock

Elevate Your Career as an Emergency Veterinarian at Central Texas Veterinary Specialty & Emergency Hospital!
 
Are you passionate about emergency medicine and ready to make a meaningful impact? If you’re seeking part-time opportunities, CTVSEH invites you to join our dynamic team in the vibrant Austin area. We pride ourselves on delivering exceptional care through collaboration, innovation, and respect—for our patients, clients, and each other.
 
Why Choose CTVSEH?
 
Collaborative Expertise
Work shoulder-to-shoulder with board-certified specialists in critical care, internal medicine, dermatology, oncology, rehabilitation, neurology, ophthalmology, and surgery. Expand your surgical skills with hands-on, intraoperative training from our surgical team.
 
Flexibility That Fits Your Life
Your career should complement your lifestyle. We offer flexible scheduling options to help you achieve the balance you deserve.
 
Supportive Culture
We foster an environment of mutual respect and prioritize work-life balance, so you can thrive both professionally and personally.
 
What We’re Looking For
  • DVM/VMD degree
  • Prior mentorship or internship in emergency medicine (preferred)
 
Compensation & Benefits
  • Competitive Salary—No Non-Compete Agreements
  • Monthly Production Bonuses with no negative accrual
  • Comprehensive benefits package including:
    • 401K with generous company match
    • AVMA PLIT coverage, VIN membership, and license/membership reimbursements
    • Paid time off for continuing education
    • CE allowance and access to leading veterinary publications
 
Why Austin?
Austin isn’t just a city—it’s a lifestyle. Known as the “Live Music Capital of the World,” Austin offers a vibrant cultural scene, incredible food, and endless outdoor adventures in its parks, lakes, and nearby Hill Country. Here, individuality is celebrated, and community thrives. If you’re looking for more than a job—if you want a place to belong—Austin is calling.
 
Ready to Join Us?
If you’re eager to practice exceptional medicine, collaborate with outstanding professionals, and enjoy life outside of work, we’d love to meet you!  Apply today via our career page or email your CV and cover letter to ann.schramm@unitedvetcare.com.
 
About Us
CTVSEH is proud to be a United Veterinary Care partner hospital. We celebrate the individuality of each hospital while providing resources and support to create sustainable, fulfilling careers in veterinary medicine.
United Veterinary Care is an equal opportunity employer committed to diversity and inclusion.
